to narzędzie umożliwiające tłumaczenie tekstu z alfabetu łacińskiego lub polskiego na alfabet głagolicki oraz na odwrót. PolGla Latin Glagolitic Translator -


is a tool designed for translating text from the Latin or Polish alphabet into the Glagolitic alphabet and vice versa. Our translator comes with features that make it easy and convenient to use: Each entered text is copied to the URL, allowing you to restore it after closing the page, similar to Google Translate's functionality. A keyboard assists in entering characters from the Glagolitic alphabet if a particular character cannot be copied and pasted into the translator. A bilingual website that allows you to switch the language to English or Polish. Try our translator and effortlessly move your texts between different alphabets!
